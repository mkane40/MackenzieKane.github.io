About Me

GIS Technician — Black Hills Energy

I am from Monument, CO where I grew up mountain biking, skiing, and hiking through trees and over boulders. I have become obsessed with indoor rock climbing now that I live in a state without mountains to play. I love to go backpacking (Maroon Bells 4-pass, Escalante/Glen Canyon, San Isabel National Forest, etc.) mountain biking, ski/snowboard, & rock climbing. Great lover of Seinfeld. I am actively enrolled at the University of Wisconsin, Madison for a M.S. degree in Cartography and GIS - Online GIS Development Program. I have a B.A. degree in Geography & Environmental Studies & a GIS Certificate – Graduated Magna Cum Laude & with Departmental Honors Highest Distinction at the University of Colorado at Colorado Springs (2018). I believe geospatial computing & GIS web integration is invaluable in todays data driven industry. My favorite GIS components include geovisualization, risk modeling, & scripting automation.
